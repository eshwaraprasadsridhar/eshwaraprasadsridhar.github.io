About Me

I am a PhD student in Industrial Engineering at The University of Texas at Arlington, conducting cutting-edge research in Human-Robot Interaction and Wearable Robotics. My work focuses on developing innovative solutions that enhance human capabilities while prioritizing user comfort and safety.

With a strong background in mechatronics engineering and industrial experience at Tata Consultancy Services, I bring a unique perspective that bridges theoretical research with practical applications. My research has led to significant achievements, including a 22% reduction in muscle activity through soft wearable robotic exoskeletons.

I am passionate about advancing the field of human-robot interaction through interdisciplinary research that combines engineering principles with human factors considerations.
