## Research Profile

I study how people interact with intelligent, embodied, and immersive systems. My work combines human-subject experiments, sensing technologies, biomechanical measurement, usability evaluation, and statistical analysis to understand trust, workload, perception, comfort, and performance in human-centered technologies.

## Research Experience

**Graduate Research Assistant | Human Factors and HCI Lab | Jan 2025 - Present**

- Investigating human-AI teaming dynamics in sociotechnical systems, with emphasis on trust, transparency, workload, and adaptability.
- Designing and conducting user studies with conversational agents, adaptive training systems, and immersive simulation environments.
- Developing metrics and analysis pipelines for evaluating human cognition, performance, and perception in AI-assisted decision-making contexts.

**Graduate Research Assistant | UTA Research Institute and Department of IMSE | Sept 2023 - May 2024**
