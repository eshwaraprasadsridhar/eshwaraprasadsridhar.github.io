## About Me {#about}

I'm a Ph.D. student in [Industrial Engineering](https://www.uta.edu/academics/schools-colleges/engineering/academics/departments/imse) at [The University of Texas at Arlington](https://www.uta.edu/), conducting research in the intersection of Human-Robot Interaction and Wearable Robotics. Prior to that, I obtained my B.Tech degree from [SRM Institute of Science and Technology](https://www.srmist.edu.in/) and worked as an Assistant Systems Engineer at [Tata Consultancy Services](https://www.tcs.com/).

I'm currently working on **Human-Robot Interaction and Wearable Robotics**, with research focus on:

• **Soft Wearable Robotics**: Design and development of exoskeletons for reducing work-related musculoskeletal disorders
  • Biomechanical Analysis: EMG, IMU, and pressure sensing for performance evaluation
  • Human Studies: User experience and ergonomic assessment of wearable devices

• **Robotic Teleoperation**: Enhancing human operator performance through advanced visual feedback
  • 3D Visual Systems: VR and AR implementations for improved depth perception
  • Human Factors: Cognitive workload assessment and task performance optimization

• **Manufacturing Systems**: Integration of advanced technologies in industrial environments
  • Quality Engineering: Statistical process control and continuous improvement
  • Automation: RFID, RTLS, and imaging systems for manufacturing efficiency
