## About

<img src="/images/Eshwara_Headshot-640.jpg" srcset="/images/Eshwara_Headshot-320.jpg 320w, /images/Eshwara_Headshot-640.jpg 640w, /images/Eshwara_Headshot.jpeg 1200w" sizes="(max-width: 760px) 220px, 280px" alt="Eshwara Prasad Sridhar" class="about-portrait">

<p class="section-lead">I am a Ph.D. student in Industrial Engineering at the University of Texas at Arlington, specializing in Human Factors and Human-Computer Interaction, with a focus on Human-AI Interaction and Human-Robot Interaction in extended reality environments.</p>

My research examines how people interact with intelligent, embodied, and immersive systems across digital, physical, and operational settings. I design and conduct human-subject studies that quantify behavior, perception, comfort, effort, and trust using experimental design, sensing technologies, and data-driven analysis.

With a background in Mechatronics and Industrial Engineering, I work at the intersection of human behavior, engineered systems, and measurement science. My work integrates instrumentation, computer vision, wearable systems, and immersive simulations to inform the design of technologies that align with real human use in training, engineering education, rehabilitation, and operational environments.
